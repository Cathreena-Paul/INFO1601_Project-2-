About This App

NASA APOD is designed for astronomy enthusiasts and casual stargazers to explore celestial events and space phenomena. Our mission is to make astronomy accessible to everyone by providing curated space content and personalized stargazing recommendations.

Since our launch in 2023, we've helped over 50,000 users discover the wonders of the night sky.
